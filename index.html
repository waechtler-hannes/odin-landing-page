What are the main components of a computer?

A motherboard is the central board of a computer, its job is it to connect all components together.

The central processing unit (CPU) acts as the brain of a computer. It mainly executes program instructions.

The random access memory (RAM) is used to temporarily store information so that specific information can be accessed quickly.

The hard drive is the storage medium of a computer and stores all of the computer's data.
